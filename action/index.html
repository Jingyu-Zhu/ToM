Core Steps Against Alzheimer's Disease

Being Informed

Educate yourself about Alzheimer's disease, its symptoms, risk factors, and prevention strategies. Knowledge is the first step in making a difference.

Preventative Measures

Take proactive steps to reduce your risk of developing Alzheimer's through lifestyle changes, healthy habits, and regular health checkups.

Spread Awareness

Help raise awareness about Alzheimer's disease in your community, workplace, and social circles. Every conversation matters.

Ways You Can Help

Share on Social Media

Attend Events

Participate in our awareness events, fundraisers, and educational workshops throughout the year.

Prevention Tips

1

Stay Physically Active

Regular exercise can help maintain brain health and reduce the risk of cognitive decline.

2

Eat a Healthy Diet

Follow a Mediterranean-style diet rich in fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and healthy fats.

3

Stay Mentally Active

Engage in mentally stimulating activities like reading, puzzles, or learning new skills.

4

Get Quality Sleep

Aim for 7-9 hours of quality sleep each night to support brain health and memory consolidation.

5

Manage Stress

Practice stress-reduction techniques like meditation, yoga, or deep breathing exercises.

6

Stay Socially Connected

Maintain strong social connections and engage in meaningful relationships with family and friends.
