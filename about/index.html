About Threads of Minds

Dedicated to Alzheimer's Awareness and Support

Our Mission

Threads of Minds is a Canadian organization dedicated to raising awareness about Alzheimer's disease and dementia. We believe that knowledge is power, and by educating communities about Alzheimer's, we can help protect our loved ones and support those affected by this devastating disease.

Our mission is to provide vital information, resources, and support to individuals, families, and caregivers dealing with Alzheimer's disease. We strive to create a world where no one faces Alzheimer's alone, and where early detection and prevention are prioritized.

What We Do

Education & Awareness

We provide comprehensive information about Alzheimer's disease, its symptoms, risk factors, and prevention strategies to help communities stay informed.

Support & Resources

We offer support resources for families and caregivers, including information about treatment options, caregiving strategies, and community support.

Advocacy & Outreach

We advocate for better Alzheimer's care and research funding, and work to raise awareness through community events and campaigns.

Community Building

We build supportive communities where individuals and families affected by Alzheimer's can connect, share experiences, and find hope.

Our Story

Threads of Minds was founded with a simple yet powerful belief: "An elephant never forgets, let's make sure our memories of our loved ones never fade away." This philosophy drives everything we do.

Founded in Canada, we recognized the growing need for accessible, reliable information about Alzheimer's disease. With nearly 1 million Canadians affected by dementia, we knew that education and awareness were crucial first steps in the fight against this disease.
